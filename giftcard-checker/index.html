Card Number Instructions

Enter the 16-digit identification code from your gift card. Ensure accuracy to prevent validation errors.

  • Input digits exactly as shown on the card
  • Distinguish between zeros (0) and the letter O
  • Do not include spaces or special characters

Upload a clear photograph of your physical gift card to complete the verification process.

  • Card number must be legible in the image
