Gift Card Verification

Verify your gift card status in just a few simple steps

1 Select Your Gift Card Type

Please select the type of gift card you want to verify from the options below:

  • Choose the retailer or brand that issued your gift card
  • If you don't see your card type, select "Other Gift Card"
  • Your selection helps us verify your card correctly

2 Enter Gift Card Details

Please enter the 16-digit code from your gift card:

  • Enter the code exactly as it appears on the card
  • Be careful with similar characters like 0 (zero) and O (letter O)
  • Do not include any spaces or dashes

Please enter all 16 digits

3 Upload Gift Card Image

Please upload a clear photo of your gift card:

  • Ensure the card is well-lit and all text is readable
  • Make sure the card number is visible in the photo
  • Uploaded image should be less than 5MB in size

Drag & drop your gift card image here or browse

Supports JPG, PNG, or WebP (Max 5MB)
